Araku Valley

Araku Valley is a hill station and valley region in the southeastern Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It's surrounded by the thick forests of the Eastern Ghats mountain range. The Tribal Museum is dedicated to the area's numerous indigenous tribes, known for their traditional Dhimsa dance, and showcases traditional handicrafts. A miniature train runs through Padmapuram Gardens, with its sculptures and tree-top huts.

Tirupathi

Tirupati is a city in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. Its Sri Venkateswara Temple sits atop one of the the 7 peaks of Tirumala Hills, attracting scores of Hindu pilgrims. Sri Venkateswara National Park, home to the temple, also contains the Sri Venkateswara Zoological Park with lions and primates. Nearby, next to a waterfall and cave believed to be sacred, Sri Kapileswara Swamy Temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Vizag Beach

Visakhapatnam is a port city and industrial center in the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh, on the Bay of Bengal. It's known for its many beaches, including Ramakrishna Beach, home to a preserved submarine at the Kursura Submarine Museum. Nearby are the elaborate Kali Temple and the Visakha Museum, an old Dutch bungalow housing local maritime and historical exhibits.

Goa

Nestled along India's western coast, Goa is a captivating blend of sun-kissed beaches, vibrant culture, and rich history. Its picturesque coastline, adorned with sandy shores ranging from lively party spots to tranquil escapes, attracts travelers worldwide. Beyond beaches, Goa's charm lies in its Portuguese-influenced architecture, bustling markets, diverse cuisine, and inclusive cultural heritage. As the sun sets, the state transforms into a lively nightlife hub, offering endless opportunities for relaxation and revelry. Andaman and Nicobar Island

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands, an enchanting archipelago in the Bay of Bengal, offer a serene escape with their pristine beaches, lush rainforests, and vibrant coral reefs. Home to unique biodiversity and indigenous tribes, these islands combine natural beauty with rich cultural heritage. From the historic Cellular Jail in Port Blair to the crystal-clear waters of Havelock Island, known for its world-class diving spots, the islands present a perfect blend of tranquility and adventure. Each island, with its swaying palms and turquoise waters, forms a tranquil paradise that captivates every visitor.
